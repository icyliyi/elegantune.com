FAQ

Will Elegantune work with Android devices?

Nope. At this moment Elegantune just support iOS devices and requires iOS 6 or later. It compatible with iPhone, iPad, and iPod touch. It is optimized for iPhone 6 and iPhone 6 plus.

Does Elegantune support external microphone?

Sure, Elegantune support three types of microphone: built-in mic, pickup and bluetooth pickup. Tuning perfomance will be improved when Elegantune work with pickup / bluetooth pickup.

Why isn't Elegantune free?

There are some free tuner / online tuner, but we want to design and develop the best and most professional tuner for musican, and our team need keeping on refining Elegantune and building more creative music apps. (Elegantune Lite is free, btw)
